# Affiliate Program Guide — TuIAlista

> You are TuIAlista's primary sales channel. We build the agents; you put them in the hands of the people who need them and earn a recurring commission every month.

---

## 1. What the Affiliate Program is

TuIAlista sells AI agents that run **100% locally** on the customer's own computer. We don't sell directly: our growth model relies on **affiliates** — people and companies who recommend TuIAlista and get paid for every customer they bring in.

What makes this different from running an ad:

- **You're not selling a one-time product.** You're selling a monthly subscription. And as long as your customer keeps paying, **you keep getting paid**.
- **You don't handle tech support, billing, or installation.** We take care of all that. You connect the person with the solution.
- **You don't need to be technical.** You need to understand the problem each agent solves and who it's for.

---

## 2. How it works (the full flow)

1. **You register** as an affiliate (free) and receive your unique **referral code** and your **personal link**.
2. **You share** your link: WhatsApp, email, LinkedIn, Facebook, in person. Use the material we give you.
3. Your prospect enters through your link, tries the agent **free for 7 days**, and subscribes.
4. The system **attributes that customer to your code** automatically on the first payment.
5. **Every month** that customer keeps paying, you're credited **30% of what they pay**.
6. We pay out your commissions to the payout method you registered.

> **The heart of the model:** the commission is **recurring**, not a one-off. A customer who stays 12 months pays you 12 times.

## 4. How to register as an affiliate

1. Go to **tuialista.com** and open the **Affiliates** section (or go directly to `tuialista.com/affiliates`).
2. Register with your **email** and your **name**.
3. Enter your **payout method** (for example, your PayPal email). You can update it later from your dashboard.
4. Done: the system generates your **referral code** automatically.

Registration is free and there's no monthly fee. Your standard commission is **30%**.

## 5. Your referral code and link

When you register, the system assigns you a **unique code**. It's built from part of your name + a random key; for example, if your name is John, your code might look like this:

```
JOHN3F9A2C
```

Your **referral link** is simply the TuIAlista page with your code at the end:

```
https://tuialista.com/?ref=JOHN3F9A2C
```

- When someone enters through that link and subscribes, they are **attributed to you automatically** on their first payment.
- You can use the same link for **all agents** — the commission is calculated on whatever the customer pays, for any agent.
- Share it on WhatsApp, social media, email, wherever you like. The more places, the better.

> **Tip:** save your link in your phone's notes and keep it handy. A sale often happens in a casual conversation.

## 6. How and when you get paid

1. **It accrues automatically.** Every time one of your customers pays their monthly fee, the system automatically records your commission for that month as **pending**.
2. **It's grouped by monthly period** (for example, `2026-07`). In your dashboard you see, per period, how many active referrals you have and how much you've accumulated: pending and already paid.
3. **It's settled per period.** At the close of each period, we review pending commissions and mark them as **paid** when they're settled to your registered payout method.
4. **To the method you registered** (PayPal or another you specified).

Summary of the statuses you'll see in your dashboard:

- **Pending:** commission already generated by a customer payment, not yet settled.
- **Paid:** commission already deposited to your payout method.

> You don't have to invoice the customer or chase payments. We charge the customer via Stripe, calculate your 30%, and pay you.

## 7. Clear rules (so there are no surprises)

- **The commission lasts as long as the customer does.** If your customer cancels, you stop earning from them at that point — but you keep everything earned until then.
- **Attribution happens on the first payment.** The customer is tied to your code when they make their first subscription entering through your link.
- **One customer, one affiliate.** The customer belongs to the affiliate who brought them in.
- **No cap on earnings or referrals.** You can refer 1 or 1,000 customers.
- **You can refer large companies.** The Premium AS/400 agent (from $299/month) is designed exactly for that, and your commission scales with the price.
- **Sell with the truth.** Our differentiator is real: the customer's data never leaves their computer. Don't promise things the product doesn't do; you don't need to.
