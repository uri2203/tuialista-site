## 1. The problem TuIAlista solves

Every company and professional has the same pain: **mountains of information trapped in files** — contracts, invoices, spreadsheets, emails, records, databases — and very little time to read it, cross-reference it, and act in time.

- A contract renews itself because nobody saw the date.
- An invoice gets paid twice because nobody caught the duplicate.
- A sales report eats up a whole afternoon of formulas.
- A warranty expires unclaimed and money is lost.

Generic AI (like ChatGPT) looks like the answer… until you realize that **for it to help you, you have to upload your data to someone else's server**. And there's the roadblock: no firm, accountant, or company wants their contracts or their clients' data leaving their computer.

**TuIAlista breaks that dilemma.**

## 2. The differentiator: 100% local processing

This is **the argument that closes deals**. Learn it:

> **With TuIAlista, the customer's data never leaves their computer.**

- Each agent **runs locally**, on the customer's own computer or server.
- We **never receive or store** the content of their files, contracts, invoices, or emails.
- Our central platform **only validates the license** (that payment is current). Nothing else.
- It's a **written commitment** and a verifiable architecture, not a marketing promise.

**Why this sells:** the customer gets the power of AI **without the risk of a data leak**. For an accountant, a lawyer, a hospital, a bank, or anyone handling sensitive information, this isn't a luxury: it's the only way they can use AI without violating their clients' confidentiality.

Supporting differentiators:

- **Monthly license, no strings attached.** Pay only for the agents you use. Cancel anytime.
- **7-day free trial** on every agent. The customer tests it with their own data before paying.
- **Specialized, not generic.** Each agent solves a concrete pain point; it's not a do-everything chatbot.
