# Frequently Asked Questions — TuIAlista

Two blocks: first, **your questions as an affiliate**, then the questions **your prospects** will ask you (with the answer ready).

---

## PART A — FAQ for Affiliates

### About the money

**How much do I earn per customer?**
**30% of the agent's price, every month** your customer keeps paying. Example: if you refer the Contracts agent ($79/mo), you earn **$23.70 every month** while they stay active.

**Is the commission one-time or recurring?**
It **repeats every month**. It's not a welcome bonus: as long as your customer pays, you get paid. A 12-month customer pays you 12 times.

**How do I get paid?**
You're paid to the **payout method you registered** (for example, your PayPal email). You set it when you register and can update it in your dashboard.

**When do I get paid?**
Commissions are grouped by **monthly period** (e.g. `2026-07`). Each time your customer pays, your commission for that month is marked **pending**; at the close of the period it's reviewed and marked **paid** when it's settled to you.

**How do I see what I've earned?**
In your **affiliate dashboard**. There you see, per period: how many active referrals you have, how much is **pending**, and how much has already been **paid**.

**Do I have to invoice the customer or collect from them?**
No. We charge the customer via Stripe, calculate your 30%, and pay you. You just connect.

**Is there a cap on earnings?**
**No.** There's no ceiling on commissions or on the number of referrals. Your income grows with your portfolio of active customers.

**Does being an affiliate cost anything?**
No. Registration is **free** and there's no monthly fee.

### About referrals

**How is it recorded that a customer is mine?**
When the person enters through **your link** (`?ref=YOURCODE`) and subscribes, the system **attributes them to your code automatically on their first payment**.

**What happens if my customer cancels?**
You stop earning from them as of that cancellation, but you **keep everything you already earned**. If they subscribe again through your link, they're re-attributed to you.

**And if the customer signs up for more agents later?**
Your commission is calculated on **what the customer pays**. Always use the same link: it works for all agents.

**Can I refer large companies?**
**Yes, and it's your best business.** The Premium **AS/400 agent (from $299/mo)** is designed for large companies, and your commission scales with the price (**from $89.70/month** from a single customer).

**Can I refer my own company or myself?**
Check with us first; self-referral rules may vary. The program is designed for you to bring in new customers.

**Can a customer have two affiliates?**
No. The customer belongs to the **affiliate who brought them in** (the one at the first payment).

### About your link and materials

**Where do I get my referral link?**
When you register, the system generates your **unique code** (e.g. `JOHN3F9A2C`). Your link is `https://tuialista.com/?ref=YOURCODE`.

**What material do I have to sell with?**
Everything in this folder: the **guide** (`guia_afiliados_en.md`), the **sales arguments** (`argumentos_venta_en.md`), the **ready-made messages** (`mensajes_listos_en.md`), and this FAQ. In Spanish and English.

**Can I sell in another language or currency?**
Yes. The platform is **multi-language and multi-currency**. You have all the material in Spanish too (`_es`).

## PART B — FAQ to use with your prospects

These are the questions the customer will ask you. You can copy the answers as-is.

**What exactly is TuIAlista?**
Specialized AI agents that help you with concrete tasks —reading contracts, analyzing Excel, reviewing invoices, querying your database— by talking to them in plain language.

**Is it safe? Does my data go to the internet?**
No. Everything is processed **locally, on your own computer**. Your documents, invoices, and emails **never leave** your machine. The central platform only validates that your license is current; we **never see or store** your information. It's a written commitment.

**Why don't I just use ChatGPT?**
For ChatGPT to help with *your* contracts or invoices, you have to **upload them to someone else's server** and lose confidentiality. TuIAlista works with your real data **without taking it off your computer**, and each agent is specialized for a concrete job.

**How much does it cost?**
Agents start **from $9 a month**. You pay **only for the ones you use** and cancel anytime. Each one pays for itself with the first mistake or the first hour of work it saves you.

**Do I have to pay for the whole catalog?**
No. You choose the agents you need and pay only for those.

**Can I try it before paying?**
Yes: **7 days free** on every agent, so you can try it with your own files before deciding.

**Do I need to be technical or know how to code?**
No. You talk to it in natural language, like a conversation. No formulas or code.

**Does it work with the system I already have?**
Yes, it doesn't replace anything. It sits **on top** of what you already use: the Database agent queries your system in natural language, and the AS/400 one understands even legacy systems.

**How do I start?**
You enter through the link I share, choose your agent, and activate the 7-day trial. The platform manages your license and your monthly payment.

**Can I cancel whenever I want?**
Yes. It's a **monthly license with no strings attached**; cancel from your dashboard anytime.

**What if I have a problem?**
You have support. Installation, billing, and maintenance are handled by the platform; you just use the agent.
