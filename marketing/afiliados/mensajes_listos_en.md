## Quick tips to make them work

- **Always personalize** the first line. A real "Hi [name]" converts far better than generic copy.
- **One agent per message.** Don't overwhelm with all 16; offer the one that hurts that person.
- **Local privacy is your star hook.** If the person handles sensitive information, start there.
- **Always mention the 7 free days.** It lowers the fear of saying yes.
- **Follow up.** Most sales happen on the second or third contact, not the first.
